JANUARY 2023

Impact

The second Calgary Pop-Up Care Village took place on January 24th, 2023 at the Kerby Centre. The event was an astounding success!

  • We served over 450 individuals
  • 233 individuals received Naloxone education and supply services
  • 200 individuals accessed clothing donation services
  • 67 individuals received free haircuts and personal hygiene services
  • We had 65 total vendors and partners for this event
  • Over 110 volunteers and 475 volunteer hours made this event possible
